Choose your own Insurance Plan

Based out of Gurgaon, Haryana, LifeSaver is an insurance broker approved by IRDA of India. We offer an online platform for insurance buyers where they can easily compare different insurance policies such as car insurance, life insurance, two-wheeler insurance, term insurance, retirement plans etc. They can make an informed choice in a matter of a Single click that too from the comfort of their home. As the insurance sector has taken proactive measures upon the outbreak of COVID-19, LifeSaver is also offering Coronavirus term insurance and coronavirus health insurance.
